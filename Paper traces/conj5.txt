\begin{definition}
A positive integer $n$ is \emph{cyclic} if $\gcd\!\bigl(n,\varphi(n)\bigr)=1$, where $\varphi$ is Euler's totient function. Let $C$ be the set of cyclics and $C(x):=\#\{\,c\in C:\ c\le x\,\}$. Write $\gamma$ for the Eulerâ€“Mascheroni constant and $\log_k$ for the $k$-fold iterated natural log (so $\log_3 x=\log\log\log x$).
Define $N_C(n):=\#\{\,c\in C:\ n^2<c<(n+1)^2\,\}$.
\end{definition}
